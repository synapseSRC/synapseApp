At **Synapse Social**, we are committed to providing a secure platform for our users. We take security seriously and appreciate the help of the community in identifying potential vulnerabilities.

## Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a security vulnerability within Synapse, please let us know right away. We encourage responsible disclosure.

Please send an email to [security@studioasinc.com](mailto:security@studioasinc.com) with:
- A detailed description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Potential impact of the vulnerability.

We aim to acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours and provide updates on our progress in fixing the issue.

## Our Security Commitments
- **Data Protection:** We use **Supabase** for secure data management and follow industry best practices for data encryption at rest and in transit.
- **Privacy First:** We are actively working on implementing **End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)** for private messaging using the Double Ratchet protocol.
- **Regular Audits:** We perform regular internal security reviews of our codebase and infrastructure.
- **Transparency:** We will inform users of any significant security breaches that may affect their data.

## Scope
This policy applies to:
- The Synapse Social mobile applications (Android and iOS).
- The shared logic core (:shared module).
- The official Synapse documentation and web assets.

## Out of Scope
- Third-party services used by Synapse (e.g., Supabase, Cloudinary, ImgBB). Please report issues with these services to their respective security teams.
- Social engineering or physical security attacks.

Thank you for helping us keep Synapse Social secure!

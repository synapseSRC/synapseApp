# Synapse Social: Home Feed (Web)

These prompts are designed for **Stitch** to prototype the main social dashboard for Synapse Social on desktop web.

## 🎨 Layout Overview
- **Header:** Sticky top bar with logo, search, and profile menu.
- **Left Sidebar:** Navigation (Home, Search, Notifications, Messages, Profile, Settings).
- **Main Content:** Central scrollable feed.
- **Right Sidebar:** Trends ("What's happening") and "Who to follow".

---

## 1. Main Social Dashboard
**Prompt:**
> Design a 3-column web social media layout for "Synapse Social".
> - **Left Column:** Permanent sidebar with large text and icons for "Home", "Explore", "Notifications", "Messages", "Bookmarks", "Profile", and "More".
> - **Central Column:** Scrollable social feed. At the top, a "What's on your mind?" composer box with icons for "Media", "Poll", and "Feeling".
> - **Right Column:** "Trends for you" section and a "Who to follow" section with small profile cards.
> - **Aesthetics:** Light lavender/white background, 1200px max-width, Material 3 Expressive elements.

---

## 2. Post Component (X/Twitter Style)
**Prompt:**
> Design a detailed post card for a web social feed.
> - **Avatar:** Left-aligned circular user photo.
> - **Header:** Username (bold) next to handle (@user) and a "· 2h" timestamp.
> - **Content:** High-quality text with some hashtags in violet (#6750A4).
> - **Media:** A 2x2 grid of 4 images with rounded corners (12px).
> - **Interaction Bar:** 4 minimalist icons (Comment, Repost, Like, Share) with count numbers.
> - **States:** Hovering over the card adds a subtle gray background. Hovering over icons shows their brand color (e.g., pink for Like, green for Repost).

---

## 3. Stories Tray (Web Interface)
**Prompt:**
> Create a horizontal stories tray at the top of the main feed for Synapse Social.
> - **Visuals:** A series of vertical 9:16 aspect ratio cards.
> - **Active State:** The first card is "Add story" with a plus icon. Subsequent cards are user stories with a violet circular border indicating they are unread.
> - **Scroll:** Show a "Next" chevron button on the right for scrolling through stories on desktop.
> - **Integration:** The tray is fixed above the main post feed.

---

## 4. Navigation Sidebar (Desktop)
**Prompt:**
> Design a tall, minimalist left-aligned sidebar for Synapse Social.
> - **Logo:** Small "Synapse" violet logo at the top.
> - **Items:** Large, spaced-out navigation items: Home, Search, Messages, etc.
> - **Active Item:** "Home" is highlighted with a semi-transparent violet background bubble.
> - **Profile at Bottom:** User avatar and name at the very bottom with a "..." more options menu.

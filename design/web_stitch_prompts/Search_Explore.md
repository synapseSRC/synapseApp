# Synapse Social: Search & Explore (Web)

These prompts are designed for **Stitch** to prototype the discovery features of Synapse Social on web.

## 🎨 Explore Strategy
- **Tabs:** "For You", "Trending", "News", "Entertainment", "Sports".
- **Visuals:** High-impact "Trends" with counts and "What's happening" summaries.

---

## 1. Explore Page (Main Dashboard)
**Prompt:**
> Design a "What's happening" discovery page for Synapse Social on web.
> - **Header:** Search bar at the top ("Search Synapse") and a series of minimalist text tabs (For You, Trending, News, etc.).
> - **Content:** A main list of trending topics. Each topic has a category (e.g., "Technology · Trending"), a title (bold), and a post count (e.g., "42.5k posts").
> - **Visual Elements:** At the top of the "For You" section, include a large feature card for a current major event with a full-width background image.
> - **Aesthetics:** High-density text layout with plenty of whitespace, 16px font sizes, and violet accents.

---

## 2. Search Results (Unified)
**Prompt:**
> Design a unified search results page for a social media web app.
> - **Sidebar:** Filter options: "Top", "Latest", "People", "Media", "Lists".
> - **People Section:** Horizontal carousel of user profiles with a "Follow" button next to each.
> - **Posts Section:** Vertical list of posts matching the search query, with keywords highlighted in violet (#6750A4).
> - **Right Column:** Related topics and accounts to follow.

---

## 3. Trending Topic Detail
**Prompt:**
> Design a detail view for a specific trending hashtag (#SynapseSocial) on web.
> - **Header:** Large, bold hashtag title with "Trending in Technology".
> - **Tabs:** "Top", "Latest", "People", "Media".
> - **Feed:** Scrollable social feed filtered by the hashtag.
> - **Stats Sidebar:** "Daily Posts", "Most Active Users", "Related Hashtags".
> - **Aesthetics:** Responsive layout with fixed sidebars.

---

## 4. User Suggestions & Popular Accounts
**Prompt:**
> Create a grid of "Who to follow" suggestions for a social media discovery tab.
> - **Visuals:** 2x3 or 3x3 grid of user cards.
> - **Card Content:** Large "Seven Sided Cookie" avatar, username, handle, and a "Follow" button.
> - **Detail:** A small "Followed by [User]" social proof line at the bottom.
> - **Style:** Material 3 card with subtle elevation.

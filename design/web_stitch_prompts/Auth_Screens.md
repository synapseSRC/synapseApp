# Synapse Social: Auth Screens (Web)

These prompts are designed for **Stitch** (AI Designer) to create a web-based prototype for Synapse Social's authentication flow.

## 🎨 Theme & Branding
- **Primary Colors:** Lavender/Violet (#6750A4 for Light, #D0BCFF for Dark).
- **Design System:** Material 3 Expressive.
- **Vibe:** Clean, high-performance, secure, and modern.

---

## 1. Login / Sign In Screen
**Prompt:**
> Create a modern, centered login page for "Synapse Social".
> - **Layout:** A clean white card on a soft lavender-tinted background (#F4EFF4).
> - **Header:** "Welcome Back to Synapse" in headlineLarge typography.
> - **Inputs:** Minimalist Material 3 text fields for "Email or Username" and "Password" with leading icons (person and lock).
> - **Action:** A prominent primary-colored button (#6750A4) labeled "Sign In" with a subtle elevation.
> - **Social Login:** A section below with "Sign in with Google" and "Sign in with GitHub" buttons using outlined style.
> - **Links:** "Forgot Password?" link on the right and "Don't have an account? Sign Up" at the bottom.
> - **Aesthetics:** High-performance social media feel, rounded corners (16px), and clear error states.

---

## 2. Registration / Sign Up Screen
**Prompt:**
> Design a multi-step registration page for "Synapse Social" on web.
> - **Step 1 (Basic Info):** Fields for "Full Name", "Username", "Email", and "Password".
> - **Password Strength:** Include a dynamic password strength indicator (bar) that changes color from red to green.
> - **Validation:** Show real-time checkmarks next to fields when validated.
> - **Visuals:** Use a split-screen layout: the left side has a violet background with a subtle "Synapse" logo and high-res abstract tech illustration; the right side has the white registration form.
> - **Typography:** Title: "Join the Synapse Community".
> - **Buttons:** A "Create Account" button in primary violet (#6750A4).

---

## 3. Forgot Password Screen
**Prompt:**
> Create a "Forgot Password" screen for the Synapse Social web app.
> - **Context:** A simple recovery flow where users enter their email.
> - **Instructions:** "Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password."
> - **Layout:** Centered card, minimalist.
> - **Visual Element:** A decorative "Lock" icon at the top in violet.
> - **Action:** "Send Reset Link" button.
> - **Navigation:** A "Back to Login" text button at the bottom.
> - **Success State:** Design a variation where the form is replaced by a success message: "Check your inbox! We've sent instructions to your email."

---

## 4. Email Verification Screen
**Prompt:**
> Design a 6-digit OTP verification screen for Synapse Social.
> - **Heading:** "Verify your email".
> - **Instruction:** "We've sent a code to ashik@example.com. Enter it below."
> - **Input:** 6 individual square input boxes with large, centered numbers and a violet border on the active box.
> - **Actions:** "Verify" button (enabled only when 6 digits are entered) and a "Resend Code" countdown timer link.

# Synapse Social: User Profile (Web)

These prompts are designed for **Stitch** to prototype the unique user profile page for Synapse Social.

## 🎨 Unique Brand Identity
- **Avatar Shape:** "Seven Sided Cookie" (a rounded 7-sided polygon).
- **Layout:** High-quality cover photo with overlapping profile information.

---

## 1. Profile Header & Cover Photo
**Prompt:**
> Design a premium user profile header for "Synapse Social" on web.
> - **Cover Photo:** Large, high-resolution landscape image at the top.
> - **Profile Avatar:** A large central avatar that overlaps the cover photo and the white profile section below.
> - **Avatar Shape:** Use a custom "Seven Sided Cookie" shape (a heptagon with smooth rounded corners).
> - **User Details:** Username in bold headlineMedium, @handle in bodySmall (violet #6750A4), "Joined March 2024", and a short bio with clickable links.
> - **Stats:** "5.2k Followers", "418 Following", "12k Posts" aligned horizontally.
> - **Actions:** "Edit Profile" (outlined) and a "Share" (icon) button on the right.

---

## 2. Profile Content Filter (Tabs)
**Prompt:**
> Create a persistent tab bar for a social profile page.
> - **Tabs:** "Posts", "Replies", "Highlights", "Media", and "Likes".
> - **Active Tab:** "Posts" is active with a thick violet underline (#6750A4).
> - **Visual Style:** Minimalist, text-only tabs with even spacing across the central profile column.
> - **States:** Hovering over a tab shows a light gray background with rounded corners.

---

## 3. Photo & Reels Grid
**Prompt:**
> Design a 3-column media grid for the "Media" tab on a social profile.
> - **Grid:** Evenly spaced 3x3 square thumbnails.
> - **Overlay:** When hovering over a thumbnail, show a semi-transparent black overlay with "Like" and "Comment" counts in white.
> - **Reels Icon:** If the item is a video, show a small "Play" or "Reel" icon in the top right corner of the thumbnail.
> - **Aesthetics:** Clean borders, consistent aspect ratios, and smooth hover transitions.

---

## 4. User QR Code Dialog
**Prompt:**
> Design a "Share Profile" QR Code dialog for Synapse Social.
> - **Modal:** A centered white modal with a violet header.
> - **QR Code:** A large, scanable QR code with a "Seven Sided Cookie" avatar in the very center.
> - **Branding:** "Synapse Social" logo at the bottom of the code.
> - **Action:** "Download QR" and "Copy Profile Link" buttons at the bottom.
